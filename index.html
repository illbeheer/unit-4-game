Crystal Collector

Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random the random number, you lose the game if you total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the when the game start the game will change the values of each crystal.
